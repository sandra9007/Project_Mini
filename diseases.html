Vaccination Preventable Diseases

You can protect your child from these diseases, thanks to vaccines! Know more about the diseases and ways to prevent them.

Tuberculosis

Tuberculosis(TB) is an infection caused by Mycobacterium tuberculosis

Hepatitis B

Hepatitis B is a contagious viral infection caused by the Hepatitis B virus.

Polio

Polio is an infectious viral diseases that may lead to paralysis,disability, and even death.

Tetanus

The DTaP AND Tdap vaccines help protect against tetanus.

Diphtheria

The DTaP and Tdap vaccines help protect against diphtheria.

Pertussis

The DTaP and Tdap vaccines help protect against pertussis.

Haemophilus influenzae type b

HIB bacteria mainly infects children and leads to life-threatening conditions such as pneumonia and meningitis.

Pneumococcal

Know about Pneumococcal diseases in child and vaccination to keep themselves safe from this chronic illness.

Rotavirus Diarrhea

Rotavirus is a virus that causes severe diarrhea and vomiting which affects babies ang young children.

Influenza

Flu or Influenza vaccination-Know about Flu disease in child and vaccination to protect against this contagious illness.

Typhoid

Typhoid is an infection of life life-threatening proportions,caused by the bacterium Salmonella Typhi.

Meningococcal meningitis

Meningococcal meningitis is a bacterial infection that effects the brain,spinal cord and may infect the blood.

Measles

The MMR vaccione helps protect against measles.

Mumps

The MMR vaccione helps protect against mumps.

Rubella

The MMR vaccione helps protect against rubella.

Chickenpox

Know about Chickenpox (varicella) Diseases in child and vaccination to prevent this chronic illness.

Cholera

Cholera is a bacterial infection caused by consuming unhygienic food and water contaminated with the bacterium vibrio cholerae.

Japanese encephalitis

Japanese encephalitis is a viral infection spread through infected mosquito bites.it is preventable through vaccination.

Hepatitis A

Hepatitis A is a contagious liver infection caused by the Hepatitis A virus.

Yellow Fever

Yellow fever(YF) is caused by yellow fever virus (YFV).

Cervical Cancer

Protect your child from cervical cancer caused by HPV with vaccination.
